SUPPORT INDIA THINK COUNCIL

A nonprofit organizations working as an independent think tank operates through contributions and support money from individuals and organizations.

Transparency and accountability is maintained through periodical audit by independent Chartered Accountants firm and skilled professionals to account and perform internal audit of the activities in the council.

Our conferences are always free for participants and study papers are free for access to all.

To fund all the activities in the council, we largely rely on sponsorship and support money.
